Why We Need
Open Source Pharma

By Tomasz Sablinski

  • Current model is broken
  • Fantastic progress in science has not led to enough new medications
  • Societal changes lead to much more open communication
  • Open source has proven successful in other areas of knowledge economy
  • Open source is consistent with virtual company model - increasingly popular
  • Individuals are engaged by intellectual challenge rather than financial reward
  • Financial reward to individuals based on outcome judged by peers
  • Low fixed cost
  • Most time spent on content rather than context and politics


Tomasz Sablinski is the CEO of Transparency Life Sciences. He is @TSablinski on Twitter.
